Why Edvengers?

We combine strong academic rigour with an encouraging, student-friendly environment so your child can learn, practise and score with confidence.

MOE & PSLE Aligned

Our lessons follow the latest MOE syllabus and PSLE paper formats, with a strong focus on:

  • Comprehension strategies
  • Writing structure and content
  • Vocabulary and grammar
  • Problem-solving and heuristics

We don’t just teach to learn — we teach to score. Students get exclusive tips and exam techniques on how to manage time, avoid common mistakes, and structure answers the way markers expect.

Engaging & Supportive Learning

Lessons are lively and interactive — not just a teacher talking for an hour. Students will:

  • Answer polls and quizzes during class
  • Try exam-style questions together step by step
  • See worked solutions clearly explained
  • Ask questions safely through the chat

The tone is encouraging and positive, so even shy or weaker students feel comfortable trying, making mistakes, and improving over time.

Built for Busy Families

All lessons are conducted live on Zoom and supported by our Edvengers online portal, so students and parents can find everything in one place.

  • No travelling time to tuition centres
  • Learn from home after school or dinner
  • Easy access to lesson links and materials

Our lessons closely complement what students are learning in school, rather than racing far ahead. We aim to reinforce school learning so students feel more confident in class and in exams.

Students only need a device with internet access — laptop, tablet or mobile phone — and a quiet space to learn.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Edvengers aligned with the MOE syllabus and PSLE?

Yes. Our lessons are designed around the latest MOE syllabus and PSLE paper formats. We don’t rush far ahead of school – instead, we try to follow your child’s learning pace in school as closely as possible.

The goal is to complement, not replace what teachers are already doing in class. We strengthen key topics, reinforce what was taught in school, and provide extra practice and strategies so students feel more confident when they see similar questions in tests and exams.

What makes Edvengers different from other tuition?

We focus heavily on engagement and clarity. Lessons are taught by experienced ex-MOE teachers, and each session includes:

  • Interactive polls and quizzes
  • Exam-style questions attempted together
  • Step-by-step worked solutions
  • Clear explanations of why answers are right or wrong

Students are encouraged to ask questions, try, and learn from mistakes in a supportive environment – not just sit and copy notes.

Who are the teachers?

Edvengers lessons are designed and taught by experienced ex-MOE teachers who understand classroom realities, common misconceptions, and PSLE expectations.

We combine that experience with engaging online delivery so students get both strong content and effective, modern teaching.

Are lesson recordings provided?

For now, we focus on live participation, as students learn best when they are present, asking questions and responding to activities in real time.

We are working towards providing limited-time access to selected lesson recordings in future. In the meantime, key materials and explanations are reinforced through homework, revision worksheets, and in-class review.

How do you support students outside lesson time?

Students receive regular homework through the Edvengers online hub. Selected work is reviewed with model answers, exam-style phrasing, and clear explanations so they know exactly how to improve.

They can also submit academic questions through the portal’s Q&A feature (in development), and teachers will respond during class or via the platform.
