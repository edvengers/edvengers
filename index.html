Why Edvengers?

MOE & PSLE Aligned

Our lessons follow the latest MOE syllabus and PSLE paper formats, with a strong focus on:

  • Comprehension strategies
  • Writing structure and content
  • Vocabulary and grammar
  • Problem solving and heuristics

We don’t just teach to learn – we teach to score. Students learn exam techniques, how to manage time, avoid common mistakes, and structure answers the way markers expect.

Engaging, Interactive Lessons

Lessons are taught by experienced ex-MOE teachers. Each session includes interactive polls, quizzes, and exam-type questions attempted together, with step-by-step worked solutions. Students are encouraged to ask questions, try, and learn from mistakes in a supportive environment.

Built for Busy Families

All lessons are conducted live on Zoom and supported by the Edvengers online portal, so students and parents can find everything in one place.

  • No travelling time to tuition centres
  • Learn from home after school or dinner in a comfortable, familiar environment
  • Easy access to lesson links and materials

Our lessons closely complement what students are learning in school, rather than racing far ahead. We aim to reinforce school learning so students feel more confident in class and in exams.

Students only need a device with internet access – laptop, tablet or mobile phone – and a quiet space to learn.

Engaging & Supportive Learning

We know that upper primary can feel stressful – especially with PSLE on the horizon. Our job is to make learning feel structured, encouraging and achievable.

  • Clear explanations of concepts and question types
  • Guided practice with immediate feedback
  • Regular homework for consolidation
  • Exam-style practice with tips and tricks to score

Simple, Transparent Fees

We use term-based fees with no annual lock-ins. You only commit one term at a time, with clear lesson dates and expectations.

  • No registration fee surprises
  • Focused, small online groups for interaction
  • Materials and homework included

Because every child’s situation is different, we prefer to understand your child’s needs first before confirming a class and fee plan.

What Happens After You Register?

  1. We WhatsApp or call you within 1–3 days.
  2. We ask a few questions about your child’s learning needs.
  3. We suggest a suitable class and confirm the schedule.
  4. Once you’re comfortable, we send payment details and the Zoom / Student Hub access.

There is no obligation to sign up after the call – you’re welcome to ask questions and decide what’s best for your child.
